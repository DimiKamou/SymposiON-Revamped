SymposiON Design System

One classical-studies playground, two surfaces: the student-facing Synthesis (warm marble) and the admin Command Atlas (dark bronze). Greek terms always set in a polytonic serif. This page is the single source of truth.

2 themes5 type roles 14 screensWCAG AA
01

Principles

What every screen should honour.

Α

Classical, not costume

Greek motifs (meander, marble, urns) are restrained and editorial — never kitsch or emoji-Greek.

Β

Bilingual by default

Greek terms appear inline in the serif, glossed in plain language. The script is the hero, not decoration.

Γ

Calm density

Generous spacing, one or two accents per view. Data earns its place — no stat slop.

Δ

Accessible & instant

Every control works offline, by keyboard, at AA contrast. The most vulnerable user hits the least friction.

02

Color

Terracotta leads, gold rewards, sage confirms, aegean informs. Neutrals are warm marble, never grey.
